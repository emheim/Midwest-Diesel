DOT Inspection

All commercial vehicles licensed in Minnesota are required to have an annual inspection. At Midwest Diesel Service, our DOT inspections will keep you in compliance with all applicable regulations.

Our DOT inspections start at $85.00 plus any neccessary repairs and include complete inspections of:

  • Complete brake system
  • Coupling devices
  • Exhaust system
  • Fuel system
  • All lights
  • Steering and suspension
  • Frame including wheel clearance and axle assemblies
  • Tires, wheels, and rims
  • Windshield
  • Mirrors
  • Horn
  • Fire extinguisher
  • Emergency warning devices
  • Rear end protection
  • Hood, bumper, and body parts
  • Wheel flaps
  • Driveline/driveshaft
